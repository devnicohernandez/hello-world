Memorable photos deserve a better place

CARTIER BRESSON is a company that crafts quality album designs from vintage designs to modern art. Using tools from traditional to modern equipments together with skillful craftsmen made the company successful providing consistent and quality products.

Products

Our products are CB Family Rectangle, CB Family Square, CB Small Rectangle, CB Small Square and CB Panoramic.

Finished albums are contained inside on either regular box or sleeve box for better product presentation and additional protection.

Album Designs

We provide various designs which you can configure when you are creating your own album.

Designs include the outside layer of the album, color, textures and other styles of how photos can be seen.

Orientation

Orientation of the album can either be Portrait or Landscape when choosing the CB Rectangle products.

Materials

We provide materials that provide quality and wont worn off easily considering a lifespan of an album.
